# WatchParty Design Context

## Brand & Vibe
- **Theme**: Fun, bubbly, playful, party vibes!
- **Personality**: Celebratory, energetic, social.
- **Colors**: Bright, saturated colors, gradients (purples, pinks, yellows, teals). No dark slate.
- **Metaphor**: A vibrant social gathering, confetti, and balloons.

## Requirements
- Maintain shadcn/ui components (cards, inputs, buttons, badges, dialogs).
- Ensure mobile responsiveness for both creating rooms and watching.
- Use extreme border radius (48px+), glowing shadows, bold upbeat typography, and playful animations.
