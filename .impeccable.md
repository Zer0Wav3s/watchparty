# WatchParty Design Context

## Brand & Vibe
- **Theme**: Dark theater vibe, cinematic. Dark mode only.
- **Personality**: Minimal, clean, utility-focused. Not a social app.
- **Colors**: Deep dark backgrounds (`slate-950`, `black`), subtle accents (`cyan` for active/MVP badges, `rose` for destructive/errors), solid high-contrast text.
- **Metaphor**: A remote control for a private screening room.

## Requirements
- Maintain shadcn/ui components (cards, inputs, buttons, badges, dialogs).
- Ensure mobile responsiveness for both creating rooms and watching.
- Ensure standard Impeccable steps are followed: layout, typography, design tokens, clear states, resilient UI, subtle functional animations.
